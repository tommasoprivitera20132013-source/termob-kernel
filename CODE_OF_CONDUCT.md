# Code Of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We want TERMOB Kernel to be a respectful, technically focused, and welcoming open-source
project. Contributors, maintainers, and users should be able to collaborate without harassment,
hostility, or personal attacks.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus feedback on code, design, and behavior rather than people.
- Assume good intent, but communicate clearly when something is unsafe or incorrect.
- Keep discussions technical, honest, and professional.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or intimidation
- Discriminatory language or behavior
- Repeated bad-faith disruption of issues, pull requests, or discussions
- Publishing private information without permission

## Maintainer Responsibilities

Project maintainers may edit, remove, or reject comments, issues, or pull requests that violate
this code of conduct. They may also temporarily or permanently restrict participation when needed
to protect the project community.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, open a private communication channel with the
project maintainer instead of escalating publicly.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to project spaces such as issues, pull requests, discussions, and
other public collaboration channels related to TERMOB Kernel.
